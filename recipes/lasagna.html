Vegan Lasagna with Tofu Bechamel

Vegan Lasgana Bolognesa, made with mushrooms and lentils, vegan tofu bechamel and vegan parmesan

Ingridients Bolognesa

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 150grm of mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 500gr of crushed tomato
  • 1 tblspoon of basil
  • 1 tblspoon of oregano
  • 1 tblspoon of vinagre
  • 200gr. of raw lentils
  • salt

Steps

  1. Chop the ingridients (Garlic, Mushrooms, Onion, Carrots)
  2. Add olive oil on the pan in medium heat and fry the garlic
  3. Once garlic is ready add the mushrooms and stir
  4. Add onion and carrot and stir for 5 minutes
  5. Add Tomato, basil and the vinagre
  6. Add lentils and 2 cups of water, let the water reduce
  7. Add Salt and let it cook for 30 minutos or until the lentil turn soft
